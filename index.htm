Brief Intro

Hello, World! This is my personal website. Here, I will share many different things. This can range from programming blogs, railfanning photos, gaming, and a lot more. This will also contain the downloads to any personal mods I may have made for a game. Expect to see OMSI 2, Minecraft, Flare, Open Rails, and more! Not only will I publish my own mods, but I will publish other mods I may find interesting as sort of an archive, just in case they are removed and can no longer be downloaded.

So first of all, why this style of website? Well, I've always been a big fan of the early internet age style of websites. I love how unique and styled the sites are and even to this day I prefer the style over more "modern" looking websites.

One thing I'd like to mention as well is the news section contains news about everything I have some sort of an interest in. These may be DLC updates, website updates, mod updates, or whatever. They are only here to serve as brief notifications of what's going on.
